Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

The short version: We collect only your name and email address when you join our waitlist. We use it solely to notify you when DiabFree launches. We never sell your data. Ever.

1. Who We Are

DiabFree ("we," "us," or "our") is a pre-launch wellness application designed to help individuals support Type 2 diabetes remission through personalized meal planning and blood sugar tracking.

For privacy questions, contact us at: contactdiabfree@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

When you complete our quiz and join the waitlist, we collect:

  • Your first and last name — to personalize your communications
  • Your email address — to notify you when we launch and send you relevant updates
  • Quiz responses — your age range, diabetes type, and health challenges. This information is collected anonymously in aggregate to improve our product. It is never linked to your identity.

We do not collect payment information, device data, or any health records at this stage.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To notify you when DiabFree launches
  • To send you your founding member discount and early access details
  • To send occasional product updates (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To understand our audience and build a better product

We will never use your information for any purpose not listed here without your explicit consent.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share your data with trusted third-party services that help us operate our platform, including:

  • Email service providers (e.g., Mailchimp or Brevo) — solely to send you the emails you signed up for
  • Analytics tools — to understand how people interact with our quiz and improve the experience

All third-party partners are contractually required to keep your data confidential and use it only for the services they provide to us.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our quiz page may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your quiz progress if you leave and return
  • Measure how many people complete each step of our quiz
  • Support advertising campaigns on platforms such as Meta (Facebook/Instagram)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Doing so may affect how parts of the page function.

6. Meta Advertising

We run advertising campaigns on Meta platforms (Facebook and Instagram). When you click on one of our ads and complete the quiz, Meta may receive confirmation that an action was completed (e.g., that you submitted your email). This is done using the Meta Pixel and is governed by Meta's Data Policy.

We do not share your specific health information (such as your diabetes type or quiz answers) with Meta. Only anonymized conversion events are shared.

7. Data Retention

We retain your name and email address until you unsubscribe or request deletion. Quiz response data (aggregate only) is retained indefinitely for product development purposes.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete your data at any time by emailing contactdiabfree@gmail.com
  • Unsubscribe from all emails using the link at the bottom of any email we send
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by adjusting your browser's cookie settings

We will respond to all data requests within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

DiabFree is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted their information through our platform, please contact us at contactdiabfree@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. Medical Disclaimer

DiabFree is a wellness application and is not a medical device. The information and plans provided through DiabFree are for general informational and educational purposes only. They are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition, including diabetes.

Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before making changes to your diet, medication, or diabetes management plan. Results from using DiabFree may vary from person to person.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. If changes are significant, we will notify waitlist members by email.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out:

  • Email: contactdiabfree@gmail.com
  • Website: diabfree.gluedapp.in
